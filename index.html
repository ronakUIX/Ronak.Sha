About Me

I'm a passionate UI/UX designer focused on creating easy-to-use and beautiful digital experiences. Skilled in sketching ideas, interactive prototyping, and crafting visually appealing designs, I prioritize understanding user needs through research and usability testing. I’m proficient in tools like Figma, Sketch, and Adobe XD, and excel in interaction design, responsive design, and usability testing. I create design systems, ensure accessibility, and deliver intuitive interfaces. By collaborating closely with teams, I iterate on designs to meet both user expectations and business goals, delivering seamless, engaging user experiences.

Featured Project

Ripple - Chatting App

Designed and developed Ripple, a chat app that enables seamless communication and easy social interaction.
• Created wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes to showcase key features, including one-on-one messaging, group chats, and file sharing.

• Developed a user-friendly interface (UI) to improve navigation, enhance usability, and boost productivity.
